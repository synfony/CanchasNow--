About La Patiada

Located in Barranquilla, Cancha La Patiada offers a modern football experience with synthetic turf fields designed for dynamic small-sided games. With two elevated pitches and convenient parking below, it provides both comfort and accessibility for players and visitors. Known for its friendly atmosphere and reliable facilities, La Patiada is a favorite destination for football enthusiasts seeking both casual matches and competitive play.
